About

Who am I?

Passionate about web and design, I create modern, high-performance websites focused on real business needs. I combine creativity, technical expertise and attention to detail to deliver polished, user-friendly interfaces. As a full‑stack developer with a strong front‑end focus, I handle both visible UX and application logic, supporting projects from concept to production and helping clients build a professional online presence that converts visitors.

A few words about me

I am a web developer, focused on frontend development, with a modern, pragmatic, and hands-on approach. I build functional, responsive, and well-structured websites, with particular attention to clean code, readability, and user experience. My background is unconventional, but it has helped me develop essential qualities for web development: resilience, autonomy, and perseverance.
